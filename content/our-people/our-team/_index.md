"Our team embodies a unique blend of expertise, innovation, and dedication. Working with such a talented group of individuals who are passionate about pushing the boundaries of AI in finance has been incredibly rewarding. Together, we are committed to not only advancing the field but also ensuring that our work is ethical, inclusive, and impactful for the industry and society at large" - Prof Dr. J. van Hillegersberg

The UT team, led by Prof. Dr. J. van Hillegersberg, consist of 2 full professors, 1 associate professor, 8 assistant professors. The ING team consist of 22 employees from 11 different departments, namely the Risk Department, Modelling Platform, Testing Team, Asset Liability Management, Cross Model Analytics, Model Risk Management, Wholesale Banking, Data Analytics Team, ING climate alignment strategy, Finance & Risk Reporting, and Credit Risk. Together, they form a collaborative partnership to advance the utilization of **AI in finance.**
