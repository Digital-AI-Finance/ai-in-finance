## AI in Finance - UT & ING Collaboration

A strategic research partnership between the **University of Twente** and **ING Bank** advancing artificial intelligence applications in the financial sector.

### Our Focus

Working together on this collaboration between UT and ING has personally highlighted the incredible outcomes of blending academic research with industry expertise, especially through our successful joint projects in AI for finance.

> *"Our team embodies a unique blend of expertise, innovation, and dedication. Together, we are committed to not only advancing the field but also ensuring that our work is ethical, inclusive, and impactful."* - Prof. Dr. J. van Hillegersberg

### Research Areas

- **Machine Learning for Risk Management** - Credit risk, market risk, operational risk
- **Explainable AI** - Transparent models for regulatory compliance
- **Data Analytics** - Advanced analytics for banking operations
- **Privacy-Preserving ML** - Federated learning and synthetic data
