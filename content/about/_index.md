## The Collaboration

The **AI in Finance** collaboration between the University of Twente (UT) and ING Bank focuses on advancing artificial intelligence and data science applications in the financial sector.

## Partners

- **University of Twente** - Leading technical university in the Netherlands
- **ING Bank** - Global financial institution

## Focus Areas

1. **AI/ML in Risk Management** - Developing AI models for credit risk, market risk, and operational risk
2. **Data Analytics** - Advanced analytics for financial data
3. **Process Optimization** - Using AI to improve financial processes
4. **Regulatory Compliance** - AI applications for compliance and reporting
