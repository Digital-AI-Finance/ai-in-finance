## The Collaboration

The **AI in Finance** initiative represents a strategic partnership between the University of Twente (UT) and ING Bank, one of the largest financial institutions in Europe.

## Mission

To advance the application of artificial intelligence and data science in the financial sector through:

- **Cutting-edge Research** - Developing novel AI/ML methods for finance
- **Talent Development** - Training the next generation of AI/finance professionals
- **Knowledge Transfer** - Bridging academia and industry
- **Ethical AI** - Ensuring responsible AI deployment in finance

## Partners

### University of Twente

A leading technical university in the Netherlands, UT brings academic excellence in:
- Computer Science and AI
- Business Information Technology
- Industrial Engineering
- Applied Mathematics

### ING Bank

A global financial institution with strong expertise in:
- Risk Management and Modeling
- Data Science and Analytics
- Digital Banking Innovation
- Regulatory Compliance

## Key Focus Areas

1. **AI/ML in Risk Management**
   - Credit risk modeling
   - Market risk prediction
   - Operational risk assessment

2. **Explainable AI (XAI)**
   - Model transparency
   - Regulatory compliance
   - Trust in AI decisions

3. **Data Analytics**
   - Customer analytics
   - Process optimization
   - Fraud detection

4. **Synthetic Data & Privacy**
   - Privacy-preserving ML
   - Synthetic data generation
   - Federated learning
