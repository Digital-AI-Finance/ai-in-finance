## Active Research Projects

Our research spans multiple funded projects and collaborative initiatives in AI and digital finance.

### AI in Finance (ING-UT Collaboration)

Joint research program between University of Twente and ING Bank focusing on practical AI applications in banking and finance.

**Key Areas:**
- Machine Learning for Credit Risk
- Explainable AI in Financial Models
- Data Analytics for Banking Operations
- Process Automation with AI

[Learn more](/research/projects/ai-in-finance/)

---

### MSCA Digital Finance

Marie Sklodowska-Curie Actions Doctoral Network - a European research and training program with 17 doctoral candidates across 5 topics.

**Topics:**
- Federated Learning for Financial Privacy
- Reinforcement Learning in Trading
- Network-Based Risk Modeling
- Explainable AI in Finance
- Synthetic Data Generation

[Learn more](/research/projects/msca-digital-finance/)

---

### SNSF Projects

Swiss National Science Foundation funded research on narrative finance and credit risk modeling.

**Projects:**
- Narrative Digital Finance
- Network-based Credit Risk Modelling

[Learn more](/research/projects/snsf/)
